Welcome to the official L3ak website!

L3ak is a tight-knit group of talented individuals who share an unyielding passion for cybersecurity and Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions. As an international team, our members represent many different corners of the world. Despite our diverse cultural backgrounds, we are united by a single love for cybersecurity which transcends national borders.

Our objective is simple - to become one of the best CTF teams in the world and establish ourselves as a preeminent group of cybersecurity researchers and enthusiasts. With our combined expertise and shared CTF experiences, we hope to advance the frontiers of security and ethical hacking, and foster a culture of intellectual curiosity within the team where perpetual learning and experimentation molds our members into exceptional practitioners of their craft. We stand united as a resilient CTF family, aspiring to reach hacking mastery and leaving an indelible mark in the cybersecurity community.
